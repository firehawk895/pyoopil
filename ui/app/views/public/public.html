My Room

Your one location to keep track of feed from all the users that you are following and a room to share your discussions outside the classroom! Also comes with a personal library to keep your files organized in one place.

Classrooms

The extension to your physical classroom where both faculty and students have their own discussions, resourceful communication through the announcement centre, keep your files organized in the library, have assignments, get graphical reports and a local search tool.

Institution Room

The one room with all the members of a institution enrolled in it! Admin even has the ability to send important information to all users by just sending an announcement. Now the entire institution can be in sync with whats happening in one place. Social, Connected, Informed.

Groups

Need your private space to collaborate with peers or friends - anyone on pyoopil can create groups of their own to help users work with other users in one place!

Pyoopil Hub

The course/group discovery module that lists all the classes on the platform be it public or private. The Pyoopil hub is the location to find all classes or groups hosted on the platform.

Account Profile

The new age calls for a new design to representing your information as an individual. Build your e-portfolio and also customize the settings for the platform from the accounts profile section.

Calendar

Don't you hate the fact that with every passing day, more classes, meetings, assignments it can get hard to keep track of everything together all the time. The calendar at pyoopil helps you organize your schedule and also keeps you on top of all activity that would be related to you.

Recommendations

Now you can easily recommend a friend on the platform to be a part of a group/classroom/or follow an individual. With an easy accept or reject panel at your hands this is the next best thing to accepting a friends request!

Messages

Your personalized inbox which enables you to message others and also receive messages from others at ease on the platform.
